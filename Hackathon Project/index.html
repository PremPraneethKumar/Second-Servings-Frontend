Our Story

Second Servings, Voluntary organization

Second servings is a non-profit organization that collects and distributes food to those in need. Second servings serve as central hubs where donated food is received, sorted, and then distributed to local agencies such as soup kitchens, shelters, and food pantries

Our Mission

To alleviate hunger and reduce food insecurity by distributing nutritious food to those in need
